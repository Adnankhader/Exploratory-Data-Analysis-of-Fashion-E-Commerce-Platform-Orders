### Exploratory-Data-Analysis-of-Fashion-E-Commerce-Platform-Orders
Exploratory Data Analysis on E-commerce order data using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib and Seaborn


# Business Overview:
The project analyzes order level data of a fashion E-commerce platform to understand business sales and revenue performance, customer demand pattern and draw insights that can improve business activities. The objective of the analysis is to understand high performing categories, analyze revenue and order volume trends over time, understand geographical demand distribution and use data driven insights to plan inventory, marketing and logistic aspects of running the business

# Tools & Libraries Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

  
# Key Analysis Performed
- Univariate analysis of numerical and categorical features
- Bivariate analysis to understand relationships between variables
- Revenue and quantity analysis by category
- Distribution and outlier detection
- Trend analysis using visualizations

  
# Key Insights
Demand is highly skewed in a few categories - Kurta and Set which contribute to majority of orders and revenue
April emerges as peak business month followed by gradual but steady decline in business
Metro Cities - Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai , Hyderabad and New Delhi contribute to majortiy demand
Weekend sees a peak in sales and mid days of the week offer low business activity
Majority of orders are in transit


# Recommendations
Focus on Logistic issues to improve delivery time
Prioritize inventory planning for higher sales cateories that is Kurta and Set
Explore opportunities in lower selling categories like western wear
Improve marketing and promotion activities in Metro Cities
